<jats:title>Abstract</jats:title>
               <jats:p>A microwave-assisted synthesis of photosensitive iridium(III) polypyridine complexes has been developed. The spectroscopic and electrochemical properties of the newly prepared complexes were studied. These complexes were characterized by intense phosphorescence emission from 505 nm to 630 nm.</jats:p>
